Love Struck

"Love Struck" is inspired by popular Horror Romance novels. It's a blend of survival horror and dating sim, where the player steps into the shoes of a single mother grappling with the loss and trauma of losing her infants father. She works the night shift at a foreboding warehouse, where she navigates both the eerie challenges and puzzles of her job and the terrifying manifestations of her own inner demons, which take the form of monstrous creatures inspired by her own child's toys.
All whilst unravelling the mysteries of her haunting visions, she engages in a dating sim component seeking companionship and support for herself and child. But as she delves deeper into these relationships, she uncovers dark secrets and must confront the sinister pasts of her potential suitors..
